My strongest recommendation is Seq2Vial.

 Seq2Vial — Reproducible mRNA–LNP development from sequence to vial

Why it works

 captures the complete long-term workflow;
 memorable and easy to pronounce;
 not restricted to one feature such as NP calculation;
 supports construct, IVT, formulation, characterization, stability and expression;
 suitable for a Python package, paper and portfolio project.

Suggested identity

```text

Python package seq2vial

Tagline        Reproducible mRNA–LNP development from sequence to vial
```

## Best alternatives

 Name                Best quality                                             Limitation                                         
 ------------------  -------------------------------------------------------  -------------------------------------------------- 
 Seq2Vial        Best overall; communicates the entire workflow           “Seq” may initially suggest sequencing             
 LNPilot         Clever LNP + pilot; excellent for formulation planning  Underrepresents IVT and sequence design            
 mRNAOps         Sounds like an experimental operating system             More softwareoperations-like than scientific      
 Strand2Vial     Distinctive and scientifically evocative                 Slightly longer than Seq2Vial                      
 RiboBatch       Strong for batch planning and experiment records         Too focused on batches; “ribo” may imply ribosomes 
 LNP Ledger      Excellent for provenance and experimental records        Too narrow for the complete platform               
 mRNA Workbench  Extremely clear to researchers                           Generic and less memorable                         
 FormuRNA        Catchy combination of formulation + RNA                  Misses IVT, expression and sequence workflow       

## Names I would avoid

 RiboFlow — already used by established ribosome-profiling software and other RNA projects. [Existing RiboFlow project](httpsgithub.comribosomeprofilingriboflow)
 OpenLNP — descriptive but generic and difficult to differentiate.
 mRNAtoolkit — clear, but sounds like an unstructured collection of utilities.
 RNAForgeRNAFoundry — attractive, but widely used terminology and harder to own as a distinct identity.
 RiboTrace — already associated with an RNA-silencing research project. [Existing RiboTrace project](httpsucrisportal.univie.ac.atenprojectsbridging-temporal-resolution-gaps-to-dissect-rna-silencing-at-the)

5. RiboBatch
